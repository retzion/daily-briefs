## Featured: `/dataviz` Skill

The `/dataviz` skill (added in v2.1.198) is a design-system-agnostic guide for creating charts, graphs, dashboards, and data visualizations from Claude Code. Instead of producing ad-hoc chart code, it teaches a consistent method: a form heuristic to pick the right chart type, a color formula with a runnable validator, mark specifications, and interaction rules.

**Why it's useful:** Every chart Claude produces — whether in HTML, React, matplotlib, d3, Recharts, SVG, or any other medium — comes out looking like part of the same design system: elegant, accessible, and consistent in both light and dark mode. It includes a validated default palette you can swap for your brand colors.

**Quick usage:**

```
> /dataviz
> Build a dashboard showing monthly revenue by product line with a sparkline trend
```

The skill auto-triggers on keywords like "chart", "graph", "plot", "dashboard", or "visualization". It handles chart type selection, color assignment, axis formatting, tooltips, legends, and responsive layout — producing production-ready visualizations rather than quick-and-dirty plots.
