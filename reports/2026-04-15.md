## Featured: `/powerup` — Interactive In-Terminal Lessons
`/powerup` (shipped early April, matured through today's 2.1.10x releases) is Claude Code's first official **in-terminal tutorial system**: animated, 3–10 minute guided lessons covering hooks, MCP, sub-agents, worktrees, rewind, `/loop`, and context management — all running directly in the CLI. Free on every tier.

**Why it's useful:** Claude Code ships new surface area weekly; most users touch maybe 20% of what's available. `/powerup` collapses the "I didn't know that existed" gap without the context-switch to docs.

**Usage:**
```
# Browse available lessons
/powerup

# Jump straight into a specific lesson
/powerup hooks
/powerup subagents
```
Pair with `/team-onboarding` (shipped April 10) — the generated teammate guide points new hires at the `/powerup` lessons most relevant to *your* repo. ([Claude Lab guide](https://claudelab.net/en/articles/claude-code/claude-code-powerup-command-guide) · [ClaudeFast writeup](https://claudefa.st/blog/guide/mechanics/claude-powerup))
