## Featured: /deep-research skill

The `/deep-research` skill is Claude Code's built-in research harness for producing multi-source, fact-checked reports without leaving your terminal. When invoked, it fans out multiple web searches in parallel, fetches and reads the source pages, adversarially verifies claims by cross-referencing sources against each other, and synthesizes everything into a structured report with inline citations.

**Why it's useful:** Instead of manually searching, opening tabs, and synthesizing — or context-switching to a browser — you get a cited research document in one command. It's particularly valuable for technology evaluations, architectural decision records, and competitive analysis.

**Usage:**
```
/deep-research What are the security implications of using MCP servers
in production, and what mitigations do leading teams deploy?
```

If your query is underspecified (e.g., "what database should I use?"), it will ask 2-3 clarifying questions before proceeding. The output includes a Sources section with all URLs referenced.
