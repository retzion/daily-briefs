## Featured: /radio (Claude FM)

The `/radio` slash command, revealed via a cryptic single-word tweet from @ClaudeDevs on May 8, opens **Claude FM** — a lo-fi radio station in the browser designed to accompany coding sessions.

**What it is:** A built-in ambient music player that launches directly from the Claude Code CLI. It streams lo-fi beats, synthwave, and ambient soundscapes — no separate app or tab needed.

**Why it's useful:** Long coding sessions with an AI pair programmer benefit from focus-friendly audio. Having it integrated means one less context switch. It also signals Anthropic's attention to developer experience beyond pure productivity.

**Usage:**
```
> /radio
```
Opens Claude FM in your default browser. Tracks rotate automatically. Close the tab to stop.
